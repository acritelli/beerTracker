About Beer Tracker

Beer Tracker is designed to provide a way to track beer consumption at the 2017 Rochester Real Beer Expo. It was created by Anthony Critelli

Answers to potential questions:

Why not just use Untappd?

Untappd is awesome! I just find it tedious to try searching for beers while I'm at beer tasting events. I usually end up carrying aroudn a slip of paper to take notes on (and see others doing the same). Having a simple interface to quickly jot down notes and rate my favorite beers will hopefully make this easier.

Can it do x,y,z?

Probably not, but I'd like to actually improve upon this (assuming people use it). Feel free to email your suggestions to critellia AT gmail dot com.
